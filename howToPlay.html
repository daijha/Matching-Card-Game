How to Play

The goal of the game is simple: Use your memory to find all of the matching pairs.

  1. Select two cards from the shuffled display.
  2. If the cards match they will vanish.
  3. If the cards do not match, they will flip back over. Try again.
  4. When all pairs are found,and the icons disappear, you win!

Your progress will be tracked by the icons above the cards.

Trust your memory. Each match brings you closer to clarity.
